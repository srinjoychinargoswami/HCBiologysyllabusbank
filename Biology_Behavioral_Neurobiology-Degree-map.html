Biological Sciences: Behavioral Neurobiology Concentration

This is the degree map for a Biological Sciences Behavioral Neurobiology at Hunter College. Please note that this is only a template and that some of the classes or requirements shown above can be taken in Winter or Summer Term

Please note that 6 of the 9 elective credits for the major must be psychology courses.

AP Credits can be used to satisfy some requirements. Science AP Credits cannot be used for major requirements.

*Dependent on Math Placement Exam Score

**Language course can be replaced with different course if you place out of language or do not have to take the first level. The class will then go towards a concentrated study/minor

***Physics 111 or 121 which are calculus based versions of Physics 110 and 120 can be taken instead if so wished
